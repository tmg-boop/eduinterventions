Who We Are

Built for Students Who Need Real Support

Education Interventions is designed specifically to help students who need additional one-on-one or small-group instruction to prepare them not only for the next grade level, but for life.

Our mission lies in supporting below-level readers as they transition into middle school, guiding them to proficiency through research-based literacy interventions. For proficient readers, we aim to elevate their skills further through literacy enrichment and advanced writing.

Our Programs

Four Core Services

01

Reading Comprehension

Reading is a science, not an art. It relies on the systematic integration of phonics, fluency, vocabulary, and comprehension. Mastering these interconnected elements equips students to become proficient readers — empowering them to read for enjoyment, access content across all subject areas, and engage as literate members of society.

Phonics & Phonemic AwarenessReading FluencyVocabulary BuildingComprehension StrategiesGrades K–12
02

Writing

Writing has become a lost art in the age of increasing reliance on technology. Yet it remains a foundational skill that many students struggle to master. Developing a coherent structure in writing is essential — not only for academic success, but for effectively communicating in a technology-driven world.

Sentence StructureEssay & Paragraph OrganizationVoice & StyleGrades 3–8
03

Spelling

Spelling is a foundational skill that has often been overlooked in today's technology-driven world. With phonics-based training and Orton-Gillingham-inspired lessons, your child can master spelling effectively and confidently — building a strong base for reading and writing across every subject.

Orton-Gillingham InspiredPhonics-Based TrainingPattern RecognitionGrades K–6
04

Math Foundations

Strong math skills are built on two equally important pillars: conceptual understanding and fluency with basic facts. Our Math Foundations program blends both — helping students deeply understand the "why" behind mathematical concepts while also developing the automaticity that makes higher-level math possible. Whether your child is struggling with number sense, operations, or early algebra concepts, we build the foundation they need to succeed.

Conceptual UnderstandingBasic Fact FluencyNumber SenseOperations & AlgebraGrades K–8
